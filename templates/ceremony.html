The Parish Church of St Mary The Virgin, Gamlingay.


The Ceremony will be held on Saturday 18th May 2024 at 2.30pm.

Parking is very limited in the area so we recommend arriving by taxi or public transport.
We will provide transport from the ceremony to the reception venue.

If you arrive early, The Wheatsheaf (the pub opposite the church) have very friendly staff, and will be open from 1pm to entertain friends and family who would like to share in a drink and meet each other before the main event.

Please note that while the dress code is formal, there is no need for hats or tails.
