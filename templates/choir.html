Choir

We are privileged to have so many musical friends and loved ones - and it is tradition in Maggie's family to bring everyone together to sing a piece as part of the wedding ceremony.

If you would like to be included in the Curtis Family Choir and Friends performance while we sign the register - please note the rehearsal will be at the church on the day of the ceremony, 12.15pm-1pm.

The choir will be performing Rheinberger's Abendlied, as the second piece during the signing of the register.
Sheet music will be provided, but you can download it yourself here.
