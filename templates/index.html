About Us

Maggie and Ollie met {{number_of_years}} years ago; Ollie escorted Maggie to her audition for the university chamber choir in September 2014. After a few rehearsals standing one in front of the other, their friendship grew over a shared passion for choral music, Ipswich Town Football Club (although Ollie often frantically googled facts prior to rehearsals, hoping to impress) and a good trip to the pub. To the surprise of absolutely nobody around them, this friendship eventually blossomed into something more.

In the first 6 months of their relationship, Maggie invented pigs in blankets toad in the hole (an idea so good Ollie said ‘I love you’ immediately) and braved a Norman family holiday; to balance this she then moved to Morocco to study Arabic, marking the start of nearly two years of long distance. Maggie and Ollie’s relationship grew stronger despite this, resulting in some incredible surprise visits (though Maggie’s surprise execution was by far the superior - Ollie got too excited) and holidays.

In 2018, Maggie moved down to London and the pair went from one extreme to the other - living together. The next four years saw them travel to over 10 different countries, both change careers into tech, survive 3 lockdowns, renovate their first house, and Ollie even taught Maggie to run - meaning that 75 of the 1065km they have covered in races between them* are proudly in her name.

*since dating

Of course, they welcomed their whizzy whippet, Leila, into their lives in 2021, exactly a year before Ollie finally proposed to Maggie on 10th December 2022.

Maggie and Ollie are so excited to celebrate their wedding with you in just {{days_left}} days!

Getting There

The ceremony will be held at The Parish Church of St Mary The Virgin, Gamlingay.

There is no parking at the church.

Please note we are providing transport from the ceremony to the reception venue.

We strongly recommend travelling to the ceremony by taxi or public transport.

Bus routes

Three bus routes serve Gamlingay village: 189, 190 and C2.
You can find their details and timetables here.

Gift Registry

We are already so excited to share our wedding celebrations with you, and are so grateful to everyone making their way to be with us on the big day.

Should you wish to buy us a wedding gift, we have collated a Gift Registry on Prezola.

We have listed honeymoon experiences and homeware which will last our whole lives, and some 'group gifts' where friends and family can pool contributions.

We are also raising funds for Alzheimer's Society, which is an incredibly important charity to both of us.

Dementia has impacted both of our families; it is sadly the reason two of our grandfathers are no longer with us, and two of our grandmothers are not able to attend our wedding.

Please consider this cause as you peruse the registry - your support would mean so much!

Choir

If you would like be part of this performance, please note the rehearsal will be at the church on the day of the ceremony, 12.15pm-1pm.
