About Us

Maggie and Ollie met 9 years ago - Ollie leading Maggie to her audition for the university chamber choir in September 2019. After a few rehearsals standing one in front of the other, their friendship grew over a shared passion for choral music, Ipswich Town Football Club (although Ollie often frantically googled facts prior to rehearsals, hoping to impress) and a good trip to the pub. To the surprise of absolutely nobody around them, this friendship eventually blossomed into something more.

In the first 6 months of their relationship, Maggie invented pigs in blankets toad in the hole (an idea so good Ollie said ‘I love you’ immediately) and braved a Norman family holiday; to balance this she then moved to Morocco to study Arabic, marking the start of nearly two years of long distance. Maggie and Ollie’s relationship grew stronger despite this, resulting in some incredible surprise visits (though Maggie’s surprise execution was far superior - Ollie got too excited) and holidays.

In 2018, Maggie moved down to London and the pair went from one extreme to the other - living together. The next four years saw them travel to over 10 different countries, both change careers into tech, survive 3 lockdowns, renovate their first house, and Ollie even taught Maggie to run - meaning that 75 of the 940km they have covered in races between them* are proudly in her name.

*since dating

Of course, they welcomed their whizzy whippet, Leila, into their lives in 2021, exactly a year before Ollie finally proposed to Maggie on December 10th 2022.

Maggie and Ollie are so excited to celebrate their wedding with you in just {{days_left}} days!

Getting There

Parking is very limited in the area so we recommend arriving by taxi or public transport.

Please note, we are providing transport for guests to travel from the ceremony to the reception venue.

Transport

Due to the lack of parking, we recommend pre-booking a taxi (or using public transport) to arrive at the church in plenty of time for the ceremony.
We will provide transport for all our reception guests from the Church to Wrest Park (a ~35 minute drive).

Accommodation

There are a number of options within 20 mins drive of both venues - the ceremony in Gamlingay and the reception in Silsoe. We recommend looking in Potton, Shefford, Amphtill or Biggleswade.

Please note that the dress code is formal, white tie optional.
