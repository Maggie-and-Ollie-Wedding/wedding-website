Accommodation

The wedding reception is to be held at Wrest Park, Silsoe, and there are a number of options within 20 mins drive of the venue.
We have negotiated a favourable rate with Premier Inn Bedford South for our guests.
There are also a number of small inns and hotels around the area - we recommend looking in Shefford, Amphtill or Biggleswade.

Transport

We are providing transport from the church to the reception, as there is no parking available at the church and very limited parking at Wrest park.
With this in mind, we recommend pre-booking a taxi (or using public transport) to arrive at Gamlingay in plenty of time for the ceremony, and to leave the reception - carriages at midnight.
